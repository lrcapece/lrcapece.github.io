How to add your photos

To bring your Weebly photos over to this site:

  1. Download your photos from Weebly (you can right-click each image and save it)
  2. Create a folder in your GitHub repo called images/photography/
  3. Upload your photos there
  4. In photography.html, replace each placeholder <div> with:
    <div class="photo-item"><img src="images/photography/yourphoto.jpg" alt="Caption"><div class="photo-caption">Location</div></div>

You can add as many or as few photos as you like — the grid will fill in automatically.
